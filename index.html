Calling All Rubyists!

The Red River Rubyists is a new Ruby User Group that is starting up right here in Shreveport, Louisiana.

If you're new to Ruby, curious about why it's so awesome, or are a Rubyist here locally, you're not alone! We'd love to have you at our meetings!

During each meeting, we discuss the latest trends and topics in the ruby community, speak about what we're working on or have discovered, and simply relax and have a good time around fellow geeks.

If you'd like to find out more OR would like to provide a place for the Red River Rubyists to meet, feel free to e-mail keith@rubyengineer.com or check out the google group at: http://groups.google.com/group/red-river-rubyists

When do you meet?

We are tentatively setting our meeting time for the third Thursday of every month at 7:00PM.

Where do you meet?

We are currently working out a location to meet for the first meeting. If you'd like us to meet at your location, please contact keith@rubyengineer.com
